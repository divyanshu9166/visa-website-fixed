Returning to India for H1B visa stamping is one of the most stressful parts of the H1B journey — delays, 221(g) issuances, and unpredictable wait times make planning essential. This guide covers everything you need for a smooth stamping experience in 2026.

## Which Consulate Should You Choose?

India has five US consulates that process H1B visa applications:

| Consulate | City | Current B1/B2 Wait | H1B Dropbox? | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------------------|--------------|-------|
| Hyderabad | Hyderabad | 45 days | Yes | Fastest overall in 2026 |
| Chennai | Chennai | 58 days | Yes | Strong for IT professionals |
| Mumbai | Mumbai | 62 days | Yes | Higher volume, slower |
| New Delhi | Delhi | 72 days | Yes | Slowest — avoid if possible |
| Kolkata | Kolkata | 34 days | Limited | Small volume, very fast if eligible |

**Current recommendation (June 2026):** Hyderabad has the shortest overall wait and a strong recent track record for H1B approvals without 221(g). Chennai is a solid backup.

## Dropbox Eligibility for H1B

You may qualify for the dropbox (interview-waiver) process if:

- Your previous US visa was issued after age 14
- Your previous visa is of the same category (H1B to H1B renewal) or you are renewing certain other categories
- Your previous visa expired within the last 48 months (extended from 12 months in 2023 reforms)
- You are applying at the consulate jurisdiction where you are currently located in India

**MRV fee requirement:** You must pay the $185 MRV fee before scheduling. Payment via Axis Bank (India) or through the USVISA-INFO portal.

## Complete Documents Checklist

**Mandatory:**
- DS-160 confirmation page (barcode visible)
- Visa appointment confirmation letter
- Valid passport + all old passports
- I-797 Approval Notice (original + copies)
- Employment letter from US employer (on letterhead, signed, stating role, salary, start date)
- Current pay stubs (last 3 months)
- W-2s (last 2 years)
- I-94 printout (if previously in the US)
- Photo (2×2 inches, white background, taken within 6 months)

**Highly recommended:**
- LCA (Labor Condition Application) copy
- I-129 petition copy
- Employer support letter addressing any gaps in employment
- Itinerary/project details letter

**If changing employers:**
- Both old and new I-797 approval notices
- AC21 letter if I-485 is pending

## Handling a 221(g) Administrative Processing

A 221(g) refusal is not a denial — it's a request for additional review. Types:

**Blue 221(g):** Missing documents. Provide the requested documents and you're usually cleared within 2–4 weeks.

**White 221(g):** Security/background check. No action needed — just wait. Can take 2 weeks to 6+ months. Most resolve within 60 days.

**Yellow 221(g):** Employer verification check. Consulate contacts employer directly. Ensure your employer responds promptly.
